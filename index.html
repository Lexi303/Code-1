AI Art

AI art (artificial intelligence art) is any form of digital art created or enhanced with AI tools.

To create your own AI art, use an AI art generator like Midjourney, DALL-E, or Bing Image Creator. Then, use the tool's interface to craft your prompts and generate images. Save time managing your social media presence with Hootsuite.

An example of AI art is generative art, which involves the use of algorithms to create unique and unpredictable visual outputs. These outputs can be based on a set of rules or parameters set by the artist, or they can be completely random.
